IPO Campaign Work

Advertising That Follows Rules. Every Rule.

IPO advertising is unlike any other category. Every deliverable is governed by SEBI, NSE, and BSE guidelines. Timelines are regulatory. Errors have legal consequences. I've managed this process end-to-end for over 10 offerings at Adfactors, serving as the single point of contact between the company, bankers, legal teams, and the creative unit.

Process

How I Manage an IPO Campaign

From the first client meeting to the release of statutory ads, each step is tightly sequenced. I have managed this process across digital campaigns and roadshow mandates, coordinating all parties on a common timeline.

01
Meet the Client
Understand the business, sector, and IPO objectives before any brief is written.
02
Prepare Brief & Find SMP
Build the single-minded proposition through research, factory visits, and leadership conversations.
03
Create Campaign
Campaign concept developed based on the SMP. Direction aligned internally before client review.
04
Present to Client
SMP and campaign presented to client stakeholders. Feedback gathered, revisions actioned.
05
Decide Campaign Type
Digital campaign or roadshow, or both. The execution path is confirmed at this stage.
06
Produce AV Content
Audio-visual content produced under SEBI and NSE guidelines. Legal review built into the process.
07
Align All Stakeholders
Bankers, company leadership, and legal teams aligned on messaging and timeline.
08
Media Plan & Approvals
Client servicing and internal sign-offs confirmed. Media plan locked before regulatory window opens.
09
Release Static Ads on Time
Statutory advertisements released on schedule. Zero-error execution. Listing day collaterals delivered.

My Role

What I Handle in an IPO Mandate

Client Servicing

Single point of contact for the client from brief to delivery. I manage expectations, feedback loops, and timelines while keeping the campaign aligned to the brand's positioning going into the public market.

Stakeholder Coordination

I serve as the liaison between bankers, the company's leadership, and its legal team. Keeping three sets of stakeholders aligned on messaging, compliance, and deadlines is the core operational challenge in an IPO mandate.

Regulatory Delivery

Statutory advertisements must be published within specific windows under BSE and NSE guidelines. I manage the briefing, production, legal clearance, and publishing of these ads with no margin for error.

Listing Day Execution

Listing day collaterals, including digital assets, AV content, and event materials, are produced and delivered ahead of the listing window. The brand's first day as a public company is shaped in part by what we prepare months in advance.
